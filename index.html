Signature craft

Layering textures, brushing light.

Our patissiers knead by hand, chill dough on marble, and torch sugar to reveal a caramelized glow reminiscent of evening warmth.

Single-origin chocolate, cultured butter, micro-herbs, and sea salt crystals create flavors with the quiet depth of night.

Dough rises slowly for deeper flavor and better texture.
